Core Modules

Everything a school
needs, nothing it doesn't

Built around the exact operational challenges of Zambian secondary schools — offline-capable, lightweight, and secure.

🔐
Role-Based Access Control

Secure login with distinct dashboards for Administrators, Teachers, and Students. Unauthorized access is blocked and redirected automatically.

🎓
Student Enrollment & Profiles

Full CRUD operations for student records — name, ID, contact details, enrollment date, and class assignment — all in one place.

📋
Attendance Tracking

Mark daily attendance per class with Present / Absent / Late options. Generate automatic monthly summaries for any section.

📝
Academic Results & Grades

Enter exam scores per subject and term. Averages are calculated automatically. Teachers access only their own class data.

📄
Report Generation

Export printable transcripts, attendance sheets, and enrollment statistics in seconds — eliminating manual compilation work.

Getting Started

Up and running in minutes

No special hardware required. Runs on low-cost local servers and is compatible with Chrome and Edge browsers.

01
School Onboarding

Register your institution and configure class structures, subjects, and the academic calendar.

02
Create User Accounts

Admin creates accounts for Teachers and Students. Roles are assigned automatically with the right permissions.

03
Enroll Students

Bulk import or step-by-step enrollment form. Data is validated in real time before saving to the database.

04
Day-to-Day Operations

Teachers record attendance and results. Admins monitor dashboards and generate reports for school management.

User Roles

Designed for everyone
in your institution

Three distinct role-based views, each showing only what that user needs — secure by design.

🏫
Administrator
  • Full dashboard & overview reports
  • Enroll & manage all students
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Generate & export all reports
  • Audit logs & system settings
👩‍🏫
Teacher
  • Class-specific attendance
  • Enter & update exam results
  • View student profiles (read only)
  • Generate class-level reports
  • No access to other classes
🧑‍🎓
Student
  • View own profile & records
  • Check attendance history
  • View exam results by term
  • Download personal transcript
  • Cannot access other records
